Last Updated: January 14, 2026

Introduction

Iqra Grammer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Iqra Grammer is designed to work entirely offline and does not:

  • Require user registration or login
  • Collect personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your app preferences (language settings, font size, sound settings)
  • Your learning progress and quiz scores

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Iqra Grammer is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for children to use.

Third-Party Services

Iqra Grammer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, your data is as secure as your device itself.
