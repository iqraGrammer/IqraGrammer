About the App

Iqra Grammer is an Arabic grammar learning app designed for Bengali speakers. It provides comprehensive lessons covering:

  • Verb conjugations (Past, Present, Future tenses)
  • 10 Arabic verb families
  • Attached pronouns
  • Passive voice
  • Sentence structure
  • And much more!

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does the app work offline?

A: Yes! Iqra Grammer works completely offline. Once downloaded, you don't need an internet connection to access lessons, quizzes, or practice materials.

Q: How do I hear Arabic pronunciation?

A: Tap on any Arabic text to hear its pronunciation. You can adjust the speech rate and dialect in Settings.

Q: Can I change the Arabic font size?

A: Yes! Go to Settings > Display Settings > Arabic Font Size and use the slider to adjust.

Q: How do I hide/show Bengali translations?

A: Go to Settings > Display Settings and toggle "Show Bengali" on or off.

Q: Is my progress saved?

A: Yes, your quiz scores and settings are automatically saved on your device.

Q: Which Arabic dialect is used for pronunciation?

A: You can choose between Gulf (UAE), Egyptian, and Saudi dialects in Settings > Audio Settings.
