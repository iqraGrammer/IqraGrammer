Privacy Policy for Iqra Grammer

Last Updated: March, 2026

Introduction

Iqra Grammer ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Iqra Grammer is designed to work entirely offline and does not:

  • Require user registration or login
  • Collect personal data (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Share any data with third parties

Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your app preferences (language settings, font size, sound settings)
  • Your learning progress and quiz scores

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Children's Privacy

Iqra Grammer is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the app is safe for children to use.

Third-Party Services

Quran.com API

The app used data from Quran.com (api.quran.com) to fetch:

  • Verse translations
  • Transliteration data

Additionally, the app may utilize morphological and linguistic data sourced from the Quranic Arabic Corpus (corpus.quran.com), which provides detailed grammatical and morphology analysis of Quranic Arabic.

These requests are made over secure HTTPS connections. Please refer to Quran.com's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

Apple

If you download the app from the App Store, Apple may collect certain information as described in Apple's Privacy Policy.

Google Play Store

If you download the app through the Google Play Store, Google may collect certain information in accordance with Google Play’s Privacy Policy and Google’s general data practices.

Iqra Grammer does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data remains on your device and we do not collect or transmit any information, your data is as secure as your device itself.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

Email: iqra.grammer@yahoo.com

Your Consent
